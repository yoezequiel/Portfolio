## Overview
A custom-built management system tailored for the specific needs of a local clothing boutique. It replaced a fragmented manual tracking system with a centralized digital hub for inventory, sales, and customer data, optimized for the store's unique workflow.

## Technical Challenges
The client needed an extremely intuitive interface for non-technical staff. I focused on a minimalist UI/UX design using Flask and custom CSS. Migrating years of handwritten records into a structured SQLite database required building a specialized data entry and validation tool to ensure historical data integrity.

## Results & Impact
Completely eliminated stock discrepancies that previously accounted for a 5% loss in annual revenue. The time required for end-of-day reconciliation was reduced from 2 hours to just 15 minutes, significantly improving operational efficiency and allowing the owner to focus on growth.
