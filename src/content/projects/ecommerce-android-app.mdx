## Overview
A high-performance native Android application for a retail platform. It features a seamless shopping experience with real-time inventory updates, integrated payment gateways, and personalized user profiles, all following modern Material Design principles.

## Technical Challenges
Synchronizing local state with the remote server in offline-first scenarios was complex. I utilized Kotlin Coroutines and the Room persistence library to create a robust sync engine. Ensuring smooth UI transitions and 60fps scrolling with large image catalogs required deep optimization of RecyclerView and custom image loading strategies.

## Results & Impact
The app maintained a 4.8-star rating on the Play Store during its initial launch. User engagement increased by 40% compared to the mobile web version, and mobile sales now account for 60% of the total platform revenue, significantly driving overall business growth.
